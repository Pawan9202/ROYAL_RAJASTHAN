Welcome to Udaipur

The City of Lakes

Udaipur, often called the Venice of the East, is famed for its stunning lakes, grand palaces, and romantic ambiance. Nestled in the Aravalli hills, Udaipur offers a captivating blend of history, culture, and natural beauty that enchants every visitor.

Founded

1559 by Maharana Udai Singh II

Famous For

Lakes, Palaces, Heritage, Handicrafts

UNESCO Status

Recognized for Cultural Heritage

Top Attractions

Lake Pichola

A beautiful artificial freshwater lake famous for its boat rides and views of palaces.

City Palace

A sprawling complex with courtyards, gardens, and museums overlooking Lake Pichola.

Jagdish Temple

A large and intricately carved Hindu temple dedicated to Lord Vishnu.
