Discover Jodhpur

The Blue City of Rajasthan

Jodhpur, famously known as the Blue City, captivates visitors with its striking blue houses, imposing forts, and vibrant culture. Nestled in the heart of the Thar Desert, it offers a unique blend of history, architecture, and desert charm.

Founded

1459 AD

Famous For

Mehrangarh Fort, Blue Houses, Local Crafts

Cuisine

Rajasthani Thali, Mirchi Bada, Makhaniya Lassi

Top Attractions

Mehrangarh Fort

A massive fort towering over the city, rich in history and stunning views.

Clock Tower & Sardar Market

Bustling marketplace near the iconic clock tower, perfect for local shopping.

Mandore Gardens

Historic gardens with cenotaphs and temples, perfect for peaceful strolls.

Culture & Heritage

The rich heritage of Jodhpur is reflected in its colorful festivals, traditional music, and handicrafts. The city's distinct blue houses create a mesmerizing panorama, especially at sunset.
