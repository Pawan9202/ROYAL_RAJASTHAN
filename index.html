Top Cities to Explore

Jaipur

The capital city, known for Amber Fort, City Palace, Hawa Mahal.

Udaipur

Famous for beautiful lakes, City Palace, and romantic settings.

Jaisalmer

Known for its golden sand dunes, majestic forts, and enchanting desert culture.

Bikaner

Celebrated for its grand forts, vibrant bazaars, and rich culinary heritage.

Ajmer

Renowned for its sacred Dargah Sharif, serene Ana Sagar Lake, and rich Mughal heritage.

Jodhpur

Known as the Blue City, home to Mehrangarh Fort and Umaid Bhawan Palace.
