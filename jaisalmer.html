Welcome to Jaisalmer

The Golden City

Jaisalmer, known as the Golden City, is famous for its yellow sandstone architecture, sprawling desert landscapes, and rich Rajputana culture. Situated in the heart of the Thar Desert, it offers visitors a magical experience of forts, camel safaris, and vibrant folk traditions.

Founded

1156 by Rawal Jaisal

Famous For

Golden Fort, Desert Safari, Handicrafts

UNESCO Status

World Heritage Site for its Fort

Top Attractions

Jaisalmer Fort

Also known as Sonar Quila, this massive sandstone fort is a living fort with homes and shops inside.

Sam Sand Dunes

Experience thrilling camel safaris and mesmerizing desert sunsets in these iconic dunes.

Patwon Ki Haveli

A cluster of five havelis, renowned for intricate carvings and stunning architecture.
