Welcome to Jaipur

The Pink City of India

Jaipur, the capital of Rajasthan, is renowned for its rich culture, royal heritage, majestic forts, and vibrant bazaars. Known as the Pink City for its distinctly colored buildings, Jaipur offers a timeless journey through history, artistry, and architectural brilliance.

Founded

1727 by Maharaja Sawai Jai Singh II

Famous For

Palaces, Forts, Handicrafts, Cuisine

UNESCO Status

World Heritage City (2019)

Top Attractions

Hawa Mahal

The iconic Wind Palace with 953 jharokhas for royal ladies to observe festivals.

Amber Fort

A majestic blend of Hindu and Mughal architecture overlooking Maota Lake.

City Palace

The royal residence and museum housing art, costumes, and weaponry.

Culture & Heritage

Jaipur is a cultural gem that blends ancient traditions with modern vibrancy. The city hosts colorful festivals like Teej, Gangaur, and Jaipur Literature Festival. Its culinary scene includes dishes like Dal Baati Churma and Ghevar, while the bazaars brim with handcrafted jewelry, textiles, and blue pottery.
