Welcome to Ajmer

The City of Sufi Heritage

Ajmer is a historic city known for its rich cultural heritage and spiritual significance. Located in the heart of Rajasthan, it is famous for the revered Ajmer Sharif Dargah, ancient forts, and vibrant markets that attract pilgrims and tourists alike.

Founded

7th Century AD

Famous For

Ajmer Sharif, Dargah, Forts, Pilgrimage

Spiritual Significance

Sufi Shrine of Khwaja Moinuddin Chishti

Top Attractions

Ajmer Sharif Dargah

A famous Sufi shrine attracting thousands of devotees annually.

Ajmer Fort (Agar Fort)

An ancient fort offering panoramic views of the city and historical relics.

Anasagar Lake

A beautiful artificial lake perfect for relaxing boat rides and sunset views.

Culture & Heritage

Ajmer blends its spiritual atmosphere with vibrant local culture, featuring traditional Rajasthani music, handicrafts, and bustling bazaars. The city hosts the annual Urs festival commemorating Khwaja Moinuddin Chishti, drawing devotees from across the world.
